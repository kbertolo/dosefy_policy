Privacy Policy – DoseFY

Last updated: 2026

DoseFY respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the DoseFY mobile application.

Information We Collect

DoseFY does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

All medication information entered in the app (such as medication names, schedules, and history logs) is stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or location.

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Medication names
  • Medication schedules
  • Notification settings
  • Medication history logs

This information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the application.

In-App Purchases

DoseFY offers an optional premium subscription that unlocks additional features. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play Billing. DoseFY does not have access to your payment details such as credit card information.

For more information about how Google handles payment data, please refer to: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Notifications

DoseFY uses local notifications on your device to remind you about scheduled medications. These notifications are generated locally and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Sharing

DoseFY does not sell, share, or distribute any user data to third parties.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on the user's device, the security of this information depends on the security of the device itself.

Children’s Privacy

DoseFY is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
